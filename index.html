Save the world with your farm!

You are the one who wants to save the world of Hyldena from the loss of all magic.
While exploring Hyldena, you will be able to evolve plants and animals into magical variations and even learn how to use magic yourself.

Key-features

  • Live in a farm in a magical world and evolve crops to higher forms, breed magical animals, go fishing, craft potions and more!
  • Play as one of 5 Races and customize your character based on their style!
  • Help the world and the inhabitants to survive and discover a deep story while exploring Hyldena.
  • Fall in love with one of the 10 dateable residents and start a family - if you want!
  • Honor the gods as not to fall from grace!
  • Customize your farm with the advanced decoration system!
