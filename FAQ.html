• When will the game be released?

    Early access might happen in autumn 2023.
    Full release might be 1-2 years later, depending on how fast we can manage to get assets and content into the game, fixing bugs etc.

  • Who is developing the game?

    Isa and Marie, two german game developers who are living in Spelkollektivet in Sweden.
    We love animals, have 3 cats and since we are fans of farming-games we are putting a lot of effort and love in this project.
    We are both more specialised in programming which is the reason the art part takes longer and is not final. After the Kickstarter-campaign we want to hire one or two artists for the game to help out with that.

  • Kickstarter?

    Maybe next year (2023). The first thing is to make sure that the game can be finished. This includes first the implementation of all the mechanics of the game.

  • Target-platform?

    PC and distribution via Steam. Controller support included.
    If everything goes well, Nintendo Switch is the next target-platform.
