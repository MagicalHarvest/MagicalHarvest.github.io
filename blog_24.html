January 1, 2024 - Games in Sweden & Game Updates

Hey together!
Hope you're all doing well and you are enjoying the winter days!

We've got some quick updates on what's been happening in January and what's coming up next for Magical Harvest.
First off, we're excited to share that we're taking part in the Games in Sweden event on Steam. It's a fantastic opportunity for us to showcase Magical Harvest the first time in a Steam event!
And: mark your calendars for the end of April because we're gearing up to release a demo for the Steam "Farm fest." Get ready to dive into the magical farming fun!


In January, we welcomed a new addition to the team – our talented 3D artist intern: Sassa. It's great to have her on board, and together, we've had a productive month.

Some highlights:

  • Adding a decoration system for the outside farm.
  • Implementing a basic dialogue system.
  • Smaller features like sleeping (ending the day) and the initial phase of basic fishing.
  • Oh, and did you notice? We've freshened up our look with a new simple white logo. It fits more than the old colors and looks more modern.


    • Next steps:
    Looking ahead, our focus is on creating the first part of the quest system – to work towards a basic tutorial for the demo!
    Additionally, it’s planned to create the system for random spawned items in the world.
    We’re also working on a new website!

    That was a quick roundup of what's been happening and what's on the horizon this month. Thank you for your support, and stay tuned for more updates as we continue to grow and refine Magical Harvest!

    With warmest regards,
    Your Moon Flame Games-Team
