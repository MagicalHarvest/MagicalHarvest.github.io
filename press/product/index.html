Description

Crossbreed magical animals, evolve crops, build relationships based on choices and find love without limitations. Play as one out of 5 species and shape your own enchanted farm paradise, while mastering rune carving, cooking, fishing, and magic potions brewing and restore the magic in a damaged fantasy world.

History

Because we love farming games, fantasy and games with a deeper story, we decided to create our own farming game with a magical fantasy-setting. The planning of the project started in December 2021.
During 2022 / first half 2023 we developed a major part of the game as a prototype with the Unity-Engine.
In October 2023, we decided to switch to Unreal Engine 5 and started with the production.

Features

  • Focus on farming and farmanimals
  • Evolve crops from non-magical to magical variations
  • Crossbreed farm animals to raise mythical creatures on your farm
  • At least 10 bachelor/ettes with whom a romantic relationship can be established regardless of species or gender
  • A dynamic dialogue system, based on choices and mood of the NPCs
  • 5 humanoid playable species
  • Extended decoration system for the farm and farm house
  • Several side activities like rune carving, cooking, crafting, potion brewing and fishing

About Moon Flame Games

Location: Moon Flame Games is situated in Ramsjö, a small village in the center of Sweden, surrounded by a forest. Unlike typical game studios in bustling cities like Stockholm, we're located in a rural area. Our studio is approximately an hour's drive from the next smaller city and two hours from the next bigger city.

Founders' Story: Founded by two passionate female Game Developers originally from Germany, Moon Flame Games reflects their dream of combining game development with the tranquility of nature. Having resided in Sweden for three years, they have seamlessly integrated their love for game development with the peace offered by rural living.
As an all-female game studio, Moon Flame Games is in the minority in the game development field, where just 24% of the developers are women (Source).

Flexible Remote Work: At Moon Flame Games, the team benefits from a remote work setup with flexible hours, allowing employees to work from virtually anywhere. This not only fosters a healthy work-life balance but also contributes to environmental sustainability by eliminating the need for daily commutes.
If the employees want to work from within other cities, they're free to do so. Of course they are not forced to move into rural areas like the founders did, but with remote work, the possibility is given.

Unique Lifestyle Perks: Living and working from within a forest comes with its own set of perks. The studio is surrounded by nature, offering the founders the opportunity to take breaks by strolling into the nearby forest. The peaceful environment, with animals in nearby, provides a healthy work-life balance and makes it easy to focus on the work. Additionally, the absence of light pollution from larger cities allows for breathtaking views of the starry night sky - sometimes with the Aurora Borealis (Northern Lights).
