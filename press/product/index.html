Description

Use magic to change a mundane farm into a magical paradise. Evolve crops to higher magical forms, crossbreed animals, explore caves, find love regardless of gender or race and help the world become more magical again.

History

Because we love farming games, fantasy and games with a deeper story, we decided to create our own farming game with a magical fantasy-setting and our own characters. The planning of the project started in November 2021.
During 2022 we implemented the farming-related core of the game and a first preview-demo.
In 2023 the rest of the features will be implemented, along with more assets and world-building.

Features

  • Over 150 different farm plants, divided into 5 magic levels and 4 seasons
  • 27 different farm animals that can be bred through magical crossbreeding
  • Min. 10 bachelor/ettes with whom a romantic relationship can be established regardless of species or gender
  • 5 humanoid playable species
  • 3 factions, each with their own view of magic and its origins
  • 1 catastrophe that changed everything
  • Cooking, Crafting, Potions, Fishing and more

Why Magical Harvest?

Unlike conventional farming games, Magical Harvest offers the possibility to develop crops and farm animals. You start with magic-less, wild plants and over the course of the game develop them into more valuable, magical variations over 5 stages. This creates a variety of over 150 different cultivable plants spread over 4 seasons.

Farm animals can be cross-bred to create new, more magical species that make unique resources available. There are 27 different farm animals in the game, all with different colors to discover.

The world changes as the game progresses. The game starts in a magic-less world, but as the story progresses, this world becomes more magical.

5 humanoid species populate the game world. In the character editor, one of these species can be chosen at the beginning of the game and the avatar can be changed based on the chosen species.

The game offers at least 10 bachelors or bachelorettes with whom, regardless of species or gender, friendly or even romantic relationships can be established. Each dating character has their own story and offers more insight into the specially created fantasy game world.

In addition to basic farm elements such as cooking and fishing, Magical Harvest also lets you create potions that, among other things, allow you to cast mighty magic to make your farm life easier.

The story in Magical Harvest is about a catastrophe that deprived the land of all magic and drove the population into a famine, since they couldn't cope with the loss of magic. 3 factions have since been trying in vain to bring magic back to the land.

About MoonFlameGames

We create magical games! A game studio from two german female game-developers, living in Sweden.

Isa is the main developer and responsible for the programming and art that is seen in the game. As an Indie-Dev with self-publishing, she is also responsible for the marketing and the website.

Marie is designing the world, characters and story together with Isa. Because of her fulltime-job, she sadly can't work that much on Magical Harvest.

Because there exists no funding for the game yet, Isa uses her savings to develop the game. This is exhausting in long-term, but we're confident to fulfill our dream!
