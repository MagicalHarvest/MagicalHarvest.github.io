Description

In this cozy magical farming game, you save the world from the loss of magic with your farm. Evolve crops, crossbreed animals, and find love among citizens of different species. Enjoy various side activities as you transform barren fields into a magical world while mastering magic yourself.

History

Because we love farming games, fantasy and games with a deeper story, we decided to create our own farming game with a magical fantasy-setting. The planning of the project started in December 2021.
During 2022 / first half 2023 we developed a major part of the game.
In October 2023, we decided to switch the game engine to Unreal Engine 5 and started with the production.

Features

  • Focus on farming and farmanimals
  • Evolve crops from non-magical to magical variations
  • Crossbreed farm animals to raise mythical creatures on your farm
  • At least 10 bachelor/ettes with whom a romantic relationship can be established regardless of species or gender
  • 5 humanoid playable species
  • Extended decoration system for the farm and farm house
  • Several side activities like cooking, crafting, potion and rune crafting and fishing
